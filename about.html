Welcome to Our Fashion House

“Fashion by Zara” is your destination for exclusive, custom-designed clothing that blends global inspiration with Indian elegance. Each creation is a treat — crafted to fit like fine cuisine, styled with passion.

Our designs reflect timeless sophistication — subtle yet striking. Just like a luxury dish, every outfit is built on detail, balance, and beauty. From fabric choice to finishing, we treat fashion as wearable art.

About Zara Khurana

Zara Khurana is a celebrated designer from Mumbai with a background in fashion technology and textile art. Trained in Paris and Milan, she launched her label in 2015 and has since dressed brides, celebrities, and dreamers.

Achievements

  • Featured in Vogue India & Femina
  • Finalist, Lakmé Fashion Week Rising Star 2021
  • Advocate for sustainable & eco-luxury fashion

Vision

Zara believes that fashion is like fine dining — it must be savored, respected, and shared. Her mission is to make design personal, powerful, and timeless.

Design Process

Each collection begins with a story — a memory, a muse, or a mood. Sketches are translated into patterns, then refined with carefully sourced fabrics. Draping, stitching, hand-finishing — every piece is a journey from idea to elegance.
